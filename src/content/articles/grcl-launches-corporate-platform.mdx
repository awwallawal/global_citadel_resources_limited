Global Resources Citadel Limited is pleased to announce the launch of its new corporate web platform, designed to provide investors, partners, and the general public with a comprehensive view of the Group's operations across seven business divisions.

The new platform reflects GRCL's commitment to transparency and stakeholder engagement. Visitors can explore detailed profiles of each division, understand the Group's cluster-based organisational structure, and access the latest corporate news and thought leadership content.

"This platform represents our commitment to making the business legible to the audiences that matter most," said the Corporate Communications team. "Whether you're an investor evaluating our portfolio, a potential partner exploring collaboration opportunities, or a member of the public wanting to understand what we do — this platform is built for you."

The platform will continue to evolve with new features and content as the Group expands its digital presence.
