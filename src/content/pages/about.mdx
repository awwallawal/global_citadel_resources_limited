Global Resources Citadel Limited is a Nigerian conglomerate operating across seven business divisions organised into three strategic clusters. Founded with a vision to build integrated, sustainable business operations that contribute to Nigeria's economic development, the Group has grown from its agricultural roots into a diversified enterprise spanning farming, processing, commodity trading, international commerce, real estate, and energy.

Our mission is to create lasting value for stakeholders by operating businesses that are built on transparency, operational excellence, and deep commitment to the communities we serve. Every division within the GRCL portfolio is designed to be self-sustaining while contributing to the Group's integrated value chain.
