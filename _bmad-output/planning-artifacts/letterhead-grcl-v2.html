Letterhead Template v2 — How to Use

  1. Edit content: Click any text in the letter body to edit it directly. The date, recipient, subject, body text, and signature are all editable.
  2. Print to PDF: Press Ctrl+P (or Cmd+P on Mac), select “Save as PDF” as the destination.
  3. Settings: Set paper size to A4, margins to None, and enable “Background graphics” to preserve the accent stripe, watermark, and gradient lines.
The yellow highlight on click is screen-only and will not appear in the printed PDF. All instructions above are also hidden in print.
